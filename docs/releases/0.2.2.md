# 0.2.2 Release Notes

`0.2.2` is the corrupted-dataset stress-hardening follow-through after the published `0.2.0` baseline.

The main point of this release is not new semantics. It is proving that the current semantics stay defensible under heavier anomaly density, replay pressure, and larger-batch corruption-heavy workloads.

## Stress Matrix

Added:

* completed `150k` corrupted-dataset stress benchmark coverage and smaller verification coverage around that matrix
* CPU profiling workflow and `.cpuprofile` capture for investigating the slowest stress cases

## Performance Hardening

Changed:

* found and fixed the major ready-queue bottleneck in `orderEvents()`
* reduced duplicate validation work and trimmed anomaly-path and metadata-path overhead
* improved graph-edge deduping in denser dependency cases

## Docs And Positioning

Changed:

* aligned the README, roadmap, examples, guides, and wiki to the completed `0.2.2` stress-hardening story
* made it clearer that the next meaningful follow-through belongs under `0.3.0` streaming work rather than by extending the `0.2.x` line further
